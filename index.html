Who we are

Love is a verb. We make it visible.

Agape — the ancient word for selfless, unconditional love — is the only thing strong enough to mend what's broken in our communities. For over a decade, our teams have walked with families, taught children, fed the hungry, and partnered with local leaders to build something that lasts.
